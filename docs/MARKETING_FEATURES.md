# Coach Hub Running - Marketing Features & Value Propositions

This document summarizes the core features and marketing hooks for **Coach Hub Running**, designed to assist in social media marketing and product positioning.

---

## 🚀 Core Value Proposition

**"Entrena más inteligente. Rinde por más tiempo."**
*(Train smarter. Perform longer.)*

Coach Hub is the professional-grade platform for endurance coaches and athletes who reject the "lifestyle" aesthetic in favor of pure, structured, and data-driven performance.

---

## 🛠 Key Features (The "Data First" Approach)

### 1. Seamless Strava Integration
*   **Real-time Sync:** Powered by Webhooks. Activities appear the moment they are saved, no manual refresh required.
*   **Deep Telemetry:** Full access to heart rate streams, GPS data, and sensor metrics.

### 2. Advanced Activity Analysis
*   **Intensity Distribution:** Visual breakdown of time spent in training zones (Z1-Z5).
*   **Surgical Metrics:** VAM (Mean Ascent Velocity), grade-adjusted pace, and custom splits.
*   **Laps & Splits:** Automated and manual lap analysis with interactive charts.

### 3. Professional Training Builder
*   **Dynamic Series:** Build complex interval sessions with ease.
*   **VAM-Based Planning:** Calculate target paces automatically based on the athlete's aerobic capacity.
*   **Template Library:** Save successful protocols and reuse them across your team.

### 4. The Feedback Loop
*   **RPE & Sensations:** Athletes report perceived effort and sensations immediately after the session.
*   **Compliance Alerts:** Instant notifications when an athlete deviates from the plan (Intensity vs. Target).
*   **Coach Notes:** Private observations to track progression and long-term trends.

### 5. Team & Race Management
*   **Group Workflows:** Assign training to entire teams with one click.
*   **Race Prep Groups:** Specialized tracking for specific goal events (Marathons, Trail races).
*   **Race Calendar:** Integrated countdowns and strategy notes for every athlete.

## 🇪🇸 Funcionalidades Clave (Marketing en Español)

### 1. Sincronización Total con Strava
Recibe las actividades de tus atletas al instante mediante Webhooks. Sin esperas, sin errores.

### 2. Análisis de Precisión
Gráficos de frecuencia cardíaca, perfiles de elevación y distribución de intensidad por zonas. Convierte el sudor en datos accionables.

### 3. Constructor de Series Dinámico
Crea entrenamientos estructurados basados en VAM y zonas de potencia. Diseñado para la complejidad del alto rendimiento.

### 4. Feedback Instantáneo
Métricas de RPE (Esfuerzo Percibido) y comentarios de los atletas. Recibe alertas de cumplimiento cuando alguien se desvía del plan.
